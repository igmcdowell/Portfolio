The recovery.berkeley.edu site is a project by Eric Kansa, Erik Wilde, and Raymond Yee, a group of professors and lecturers at the UC Berkeley School of Information. My role was to design and build the site, write some of the content, and generate example data for some of the technical demonstrations. This was a paid contract position from 2008. Complete source code is available on request.
