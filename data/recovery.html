The recovery.berkeley.edu site is a project by Eric Kansa, Erik Wilde, and Raymond Yee, a group of professors and lecturers at the UC Berkeley School of Information. My role was to design and build the site, write some of the content, and generate example data for some of the technical demonstrations. This was a paid contract position from 2009. Complete source code is available on request.

This site was intended as a response to quick-moving political changes from the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009. Given the quick time-table, planning activities were limited to stylistic consultation and card sorting.
